Miniature Autonomous Submarine

AI-powered underwater inspection

An affordable, autonomous submarine designed for underwater structural monitoring and environmental assessment. This dashboard brings together live telemetry, AI crack detection, and mission logs in a single view.

Project Highlights

  • Real-time telemetry streaming from onboard sensors.
  • AI-based crack detection on underwater structures.
  • Mission log viewer with full sensor history.
  • Interactive map view for submarine position.
